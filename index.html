nyrene monforte is a video journalist and writer interested in imagining visions of an equitable world. she covers social movements, community art spaces and other topics at the intersections of culture, identity and politics.

she has interned at the washington post and was a 2023 aaja voices fellow.

nyrene is studying journalism with a minor in asian american studies at the university of maryland, college park. she is a frederick douglass scholarship recipient and a proud community college grad.
